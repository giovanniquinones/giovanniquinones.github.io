I taught this workshop in summer 2020 as a temporary instructor for the UCLA QCBio Collaboratory. The workshop was held online due to the pandemic. The material consists of a hands-on tutorial on the [Best Practices](https://gatk.broadinstitute.org/hc/en-us/articles/360035535932-Germline-short-variant-discovery-SNPs-Indels) protocol from GATK for small germline variant calling.
